IEEE Student Congress - 2021


The objective of this event is to give students the opportunity to understand different aspects of the professional domain ranging from freelancing to product development. Additionally, it is to upskill the students of the section and especially sub-sections through numerous workshops and webinars. The competitions are a medium to engage the students and give them a platform to showcase their talents and provide an opportunity to apply their skills while ensuring. The event is jointly organized by IEEE Mysore Subsection and IEEE Bangalore Section Student Coordination team.
